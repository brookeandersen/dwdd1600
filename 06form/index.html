Saddle Up Scoops was started by two friends, Brooke & Paige. Growing up in Dallas, Texas, Brooke and Paige were constantly craving Ice Cream in the heat of the summer. This is when the idea for Saddle Up Scoops was born! After years of testing out new flavors and combinations, they were finally able to take their southern charm to Boston where they opened their first storefront.
